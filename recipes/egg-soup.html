Egg Soup

Description

Ever feeling cold at night? This is perfect for EGG SOUP TIME! This soup will warm you through entire night, and you can also eat this when you got fever.

Ingredients

  • An egg
  • 250 ml of water
  • Enough salt and MSG

Steps

  1. Pour out the water into the pot.
  2. Once it boils, crack the egg and put it into the pot while whisking the water using fork or whisker
  3. Add enough salt and MSG to the mixed egg and water
  4. Wait for 15 minutes, and done! You can put the soup into your favourite bowl!
