Fried Egg Noodle

Description

Have you ever been bored of eating instant ramen? And want to try something new? This must be the recipes you've been looking for.

Ingredients

  • An egg
  • 2 tablespoon of all-purpose flour
  • Any instant ramen you like

Steps

  1. Boil the noodle in the pot for 5 minutes
  2. Crack the egg and put it into a bowl
  3. Add 2 tablespoon of all-purpose flour to the bowl
  4. Add the boiled noodle to the bowl
  5. Put all the spices of the ramen into the bowl as well
  6. Whisk it all together
  7. Heat the pan with oil on it
  8. Pour what's within the bowl into the pan
  9. Fry it for 7 minutes
  10. Don't forget to flip both sides
